Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 Aug 2025

SnowDrop (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a tipping extension and web app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we use and protect it.

What we collect

  • Wallet data: public wallet address(es), network, transaction hashes and amounts related to tips, drops, deposits, and claims.
  • Social handles: your X/Twitter handle and tweet IDs you interact with inside SnowDrop.
  • Account & app data: basic profile metadata from sign‑in (e.g., Supabase auth ID), extension ID for pairing, and app settings.
  • Technical logs: timestamps, browser type/version, and error logs to debug features and prevent fraud/abuse.

We do not collect your private keys or seed phrases. Transactions are signed in your wallet or by an explicit relayer flow you approve.

Where data lives

  • On‑chain: blockchain transactions are public and immutable by design.
  • Supabase (managed Postgres): we store app data such as tips, drops, wallet ↔ handle mapping, and logs.
  • Twitter/X API: used to read handles/tweet IDs and to send reply notifications when enabled.

How we use data

  • To operate core features: create drops, tip from jar, claim tips, show balances/history.
  • To detect and prevent fraud/abuse and to maintain service integrity.
  • To provide user support and fix bugs.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share only with service providers necessary to run SnowDrop (e.g., Supabase) under appropriate terms, or if required by law.

Permissions & extension behavior

The Chrome extension requests the minimum permissions needed to inject UI on X (twitter.com) and interact with your connected wallet. It never accesses private keys.

Data retention

We retain Supabase records for as long as needed to operate the service and comply with law. On‑chain data cannot be altered or deleted by us.

Your choices

  • You can disconnect your wallet and stop using the extension at any time.
  • You may request deletion of Supabase‑stored personal data (not blockchain data) by emailing us.

International & legal

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR/CCPA equivalents. Contact us to exercise access, correction, or deletion for data we control off‑chain.

Children

SnowDrop is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect such data.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll post the new date above and, when material, provide a prominent notice.
